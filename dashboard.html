About the Drama

The Penthouse (펜트하우스: War in Life) is a South Korean drama series filled with revenge, betrayal, and shocking twists. Set in the luxurious Hera Palace, it tells the story of three women whose ambitions and secrets lead to intense conflicts.

Main Characters

Shim Su-ryeon

The elegant woman of Hera Palace with a hidden past.

Oh Yoon-hee

A strong mother fighting to give her daughter a better life.

Cheon Seo-jin

An ambitious soprano who will do anything for power.

Co-Actors

Joo Dan-tae

The cunning real estate tycoon of Hera Palace.

Ha Yoon-cheol

A skilled doctor struggling with his marriage and ambitions.

Kang Ma-ri

A nouveau riche mother desperate to hide her past.

Lee Gyu-jin

A corrupt lawyer who schemes with the Hera Palace elite.

Go Sang-ah

A social climber obsessed with status and luxury.
